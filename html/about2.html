SwapIt was launched in early 2016 as a simpler way to swap books online. Created by the students of PES University, SwapIt was developed to provide a new and user-friendly experience to easily swap books with other like-minded people.
We hope to revolutionize the way people get paperbacks online, saving members hundreds, and even thousands of rupees a year. In addition to the savings,SwapIt hopes to offers members,in future,the convenience of free home delivery and the largest selection of current and past titles. SwapIt brings Hardcover and Textbooks to the mix, including hard to find, out of print titles. Being a SwapIt member makes it a simple one click process to subscribe to wide range of books available out there and provides you discounts to your online postage for swapping books as well as other integrated account features.
